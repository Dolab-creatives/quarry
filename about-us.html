Safeer. K obtained Environmental Clearance vide No. EC24B001KL121917 dated 17/12/2024 (Proposal No. SIA/KL/MIN/436281/2023, File No. 2373/EC1/2023/SEIAA) for the Granite Building Stone Quarry Project covering an area of 0.9796 Ha at Block No. 77, Re-Sy. No. 325/1-1 in Thiruvali Village, Nilambur Taluk, Malappuram District, Kerala.

The Environmental Clearance has been granted initially for a period of 3 years from the date of execution of the mine lease. The total life of the project is 3 years, and the project is subject to review by SEAC at the end of this period.

The project is estimated to have an annual production capacity of 79,818.7 MT for the life of mine of 3 years.

The total estimated project cost is ₹1,20,00,000/-.
